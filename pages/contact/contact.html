Contact Us

Hello, we are researchers from Dartmouth College who are interested in how and why some people perceive faces as distorted. We hope to gather information that may help people who have this problem.

If you are interested in learning more about our research, please provide your contact information below and a member of our research team will be in touch to provide more information and to see if you are eligible to participate. Participation in research is voluntary, and you can choose to withdraw at any time.
